SCREEN 1: Splash / launch

Finding 1-1:
  Severity: P2
  File: app/_layout.tsx:119-121
  What: SplashScreen.hideAsync() is called IMMEDIATELY on mount
    (useEffect at line 119), AND also in the init() function (line 109),
    AND after a 5-second hard deadline timer (line 132). The immediate
    hideAsync on mount means the user sees the RN loading overlay
    (navy + spinner) instead of the native splash — the branded splash
    disappears almost instantly, replaced by a generic spinner. This
    eliminates the splash branding moment entirely.
  Benchmark: Uber shows its branded splash for 1-2 seconds minimum
    before transitioning. Apple Health uses the native splash until
    content is ready. Strava shows the orange logo for a deliberate
    branded moment.
  Fix: Remove the immediate hideAsync at line 119-121. Keep the splash
    visible until init completes (line 109) or the 5s deadline. The
    init effect at line 93 already calls hideAsync when done. Remove:
    useEffect(() => { SplashScreen.hideAsync().catch(() => {}); }, []);
  Evidence: [CODE]
